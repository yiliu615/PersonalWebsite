Yi Liu

Ph.D. Candidate in Biostatistics, Emory University

I am a biostatistics researcher focusing on survival and longitudinal data analysis, latent variable methods, frailty models, clinical trials, and quantile regression. My work combines methodological development with clinical collaborations in oncology and related biomedical studies.
