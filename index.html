Ph.D. Candidate in Biostatistics, Emory University

I am currently a Ph.D. candidate in Biostatistics at Emory University, advised by Dr. Limin Peng. My current research focuses on survival and longitudinal data analysis, with active work in quantile regression, frailty modeling, and clinical trial methodology.

I am also an incoming postdoctoral researcher (Fall 2026) in University of Michigan, coadvised by Dr. Dongling Zeng and Dr. Yuanjia Wang. I am interested in exploring reinforcement learning for dynamic treatment regimes and AI-driven methods in Biomedical research. Feel free to email me if any collaboration opportunities!
